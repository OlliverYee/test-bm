BMIS Snapshot

The Building Materials Information System uses information in an asbestos register to automatically create a live asbestos management plan for the workplace. The management plans are designed to meet regulatory requirements and the approved code of practice.

BMIS acts as a repository for all asbestos information relevant to the building. Air monitoring data, clearance certificates for removed asbestos, workers asbestos certificates and task analysis are all managed in the system.

Asbestos Repository

Asbestos register with floor plan mapping. Upload surveys, air monitoring information and removal plans.

Risk management tools to assist with removing asbestos and asbestos assessments.

Why BMIS?

BMIS has been developed in New Zealand to help you meet New Zealand asbestos regulatory requirements. Other management systems may only offer an asbestos register holding system, so why not have more?
